Media for Android

I have a large media collection, albums on a Logitech Media Server and lots of downloaded (and downloading) podcasts. It's nice easily to move these media files to my Android phone for easy playback. The audio quality of the phone isn't terrific, but that's not so important for non-musical material. And when I want to listen to high quality, I use my old Headamp pico DAC/Amp and some Etymotic earphones.

Youtube Lectures and Presentations

Some of my favorite listening are university lectures. Often these lectures are posted on Youtube. I suppose that I could audit the Youtube posts directly, but I prefer to download the videos, convert them to audio and upload them to my phone as albums. This is pretty easy with youtube-dl. I've had difficutly getting the playlist feature to work, but it is almost as easy to to feed this script a list of videoID tags.
