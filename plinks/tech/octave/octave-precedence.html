Octave operators presented in order of increasing precedence; later entries bind more tightly than the earlier ones. The source for this page is: the language reference.

Operator Description Associativity
= += -= *= /= \= ^= .*= ./= .\= .^= |= &= Assignment Right to Left
|| Logical Or Left to Right, Short Circuits
&& Logical And Left to Right, Short Circuits
| Element-wise Or Left to Right
&& Element-wise And Left to Right
< <= == >= > != ~= Logical Comparison Left to Right
: :: Slicing and Vector Generation Left to Right
+ - Addition and Subtraction Left to Right
* / \ .* ./ .\ Multiplication and Division Left to Right
+ - ◾ ++ -- ◾ ~ ! Uniary Plus and Minus ◾ Prefix Increment and Decreent ◾ Logical Negation Left to Right, Uniary
' .' ^ ** .^ .** Transposition and Exponentiation Left to Right
++ -- Postfix Increment and Decrement Right to Left
() ◾ {} ◾ . Function Call ◾ Array Indexing ◾ Structure Indexing Left to Right
