My work as a Clinical Database Manager entailed installation and administration of a Sybase database server and of the Clindex clinical trial management software that ran on this server. I wrote standard operating procedures (SOPs) to ensure that the work was done consistently and according to the applicable regulations. Particularly challenging was the need to meet the then recently implemented federal regulations for electronic clinical trial records, Title 21 CFR Part 11 of the Code of Federal Regulations.
