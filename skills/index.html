Measurement, Analysis and Reporting

Clinical Databases

My work as a Clinical Database Manager entailed installation and administration of a Sybase database server and of the Clindex clinical trial management software that ran on this server. This work included designing database tables and forms to support data entry and monitoring by the clinical trial personnel, all in accordance with the clinical trial protocols. I wrote standard operating procedures (SOPs) to ensure that the work was done consistently and according to the applicable regulations. Particularly challenging was the need to meet the then recently implemented federal regulations for electronic clinical trial records, Title 21 CFR Part 11 of the Code of Federal Regulations. The FDA examined my implementation of Uromedica's clinical trial database during a Biomedical Monitoring (BIMO) inspection and found it to be sound, fully compliant with the regulations.
